Together, We're Saving Lives — One Paw at a Time

Welcome to Arkline animal shelter, where families grow, friendships form, and second chances begin. As a community-powered, nonprofit animal shelter, we rescue pets in need and help them find loving forever homes — often with families just like yours. Whether you're here to adopt your next furry family member, lend a hand as a volunteer, or support our mission through donations, you’re making a real difference. With your help, we’re not just saving animals — we’re building a more compassionate community.

Our Mission

At Arkline Animal Shelter, our mission is to provide a safe haven for animals in need, ensuring they receive the care, love, and respect they deserve. We are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals while promoting responsible pet ownership and community awareness about animal welfare.
