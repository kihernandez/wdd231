Site Name

Arkline Animal Shelter

The name reflects the mission of the site, which is to provide a safe place for animals in need like Noah's Ark, while also incorporating the concept of a lifeline for these animals.

Site Purpose

The goal and purpose of this website is to support, rescue, rehabilitate and create awareness of homeless, abandoned and mistreated animals. Also to provide a way for the community to sign up to volunteer to our animal shelter or to adopt any animal.

Scenarios

1. How can I take action and Volunteer?

-John is a 25-year-old college student who loves animals and wants to give back to the community. He has some free time on weekends and is looking for ways to volunteer at a local animal shelter.

2. How can I adopt a pet?

-Sarah is a 32-year-old marketing manager who recently moved into a house with a fenced yard. She's been thinking about adopting a dog for companionship and wants to support local rescues rather than buying from breeders.
